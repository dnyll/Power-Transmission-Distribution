# Power-Transmission-and-Distribution-Lab

⚡ **Power Transmission & Distribution Lab Portfolio**

A comprehensive collection of **Power Transmission and Distribution Laboratory** experiments, simulations, practical observations, and technical analyses developed throughout the **EE-401L Power Transmission & Distribution Lab** course. This repository demonstrates hands-on understanding of power-system behavior using simulation tools, laboratory trainers, SCADA systems, power-quality measurements, transformer studies, and transmission-line analysis.

## 📖 About the Repository

This repository serves as a practical learning resource for students and electrical engineering learners interested in power transmission, power distribution, grid behavior, and power-system simulation.

The labs are designed to provide hands-on experience with:

- PSCAD/EMTDC simulation
- Electromagnetic transient analysis
- Single-phase and three-phase AC circuit behavior
- Power profile and power factor analysis
- Transmission line performance
- Ferranti effect verification
- Voltage regulation improvement
- Shunt reactor compensation
- Power quality measurements
- Transformer loading behavior
- Tap-changing and phase-shifting transformers
- HVDC power-flow studies
- Grid station operation and field observation

Each laboratory module focuses on a specific power-system concept and includes objectives, apparatus/software, procedure, results, observations, and engineering interpretation.

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By completing these laboratory exercises, students will be able to:

- ✔ Understand basic and advanced transmission-line behavior
- ✔ Simulate electrical transients using PSCAD/EMTDC
- ✔ Analyze voltage, current, active power, reactive power, and apparent power
- ✔ Study power factor behavior in single-phase and three-phase AC systems
- ✔ Verify the Ferranti effect in medium and long transmission lines
- ✔ Improve voltage regulation using shunt reactor compensation
- ✔ Measure and interpret power quality parameters
- ✔ Analyze induction motor loading and power factor correction
- ✔ Understand transformer behavior under different loading conditions
- ✔ Study tap-changing and phase-shifting transformer effects
- ✔ Perform HVDC transmission-line power-flow analysis using PowerWorld
- ✔ Relate laboratory results with real-world grid station operation

## 📚 Laboratory Modules

### 🔹 Lab 01 – Familiarization with PSCAD/EMTDC

- Introduction to PSCAD/EMTDC
- Construction of a basic RLC circuit
- Current and capacitor-voltage waveform observation
- Electromagnetic transient response analysis
- Damping and oscillatory response interpretation

### 🔹 Lab 02 – Power Profile and Power Factor in AC Circuits

- Single-phase and three-phase AC circuit simulation
- Active, reactive, and apparent power analysis
- Power factor calculation and interpretation
- PSCAD-based waveform and power profile observation

### 🔹 Lab 03 – Three-Phase Transmission Line Behavior

- Practical transmission-line behavior study
- Lucas-Nülle trainer operation
- SCADA-based measurement and monitoring
- Voltage, current, and power behavior under different line conditions

### 🔹 Lab 04 – Ferranti Effect Verification

- Ferranti effect in 150 km and 300 km transmission lines
- Capacitive load condition analysis
- Receiving-end voltage rise observation
- Medium and long transmission-line comparison

### 🔹 Lab 05 – Voltage Regulation Using Shunt Reactor

- Transmission-line voltage regulation study
- Shunt reactor compensation
- Reactive power absorption
- Improvement of receiving-end voltage profile

### 🔹 Lab 06 – Power Quality Measurement of Synchronous Motor

- Three-phase synchronous motor no-load operation
- Power quality parameter measurement
- Voltage, current, power factor, and harmonic observation
- Lucas-Nülle trainer-based motor analysis

### 🔹 Lab 07 – Induction Motor Load Analysis and Power Factor Correction

- Three-phase induction motor loading
- Power factor measurement under different load conditions
- Capacitor-based power factor correction
- Energy management system monitoring

### 🔹 Lab 08 – Visit to NGCP Nowshera 220 kV Grid Station

- Practical grid station observation
- 220 kV transmission and substation equipment study
- Transformer, busbar, circuit breaker, and protection system overview
- Real-world power transmission and distribution understanding

### 🔹 Lab 09 – Transformer Behavior Under Different Loading Conditions

- Transformer loading study
- Voltage and current behavior under different loads
- Efficiency and regulation concepts
- Practical transformer performance interpretation

### 🔹 Lab 10 – Tap-Changer and Phase-Shifter Transformers

- Tap-changing transformer study
- Phase-shifter transformer operation
- Transmission-system performance improvement
- Voltage control and power-flow direction analysis

### 🔹 Lab 11 – HVDC Transmission Lines in Power Flow Calculations

- HVDC transmission-line modeling
- PowerWorld Simulator implementation
- AC/DC power-flow comparison
- HVDC impact on transmission-system performance
